# D10: Industry-Specific Requirements & Competitor Analysis

**Priority:** 10th | **Depends on:** All prior dimensions

## Research Questions — Part A: Job Description Analysis

1. Pull 5-10 current AI governance / AI risk / responsible AI job descriptions from target companies
2. Extract most commonly recurring keywords and requirements
3. Identify patterns in how roles describe the ideal candidate
4. Map: requirements Matthew meets, partially meets, and hard gaps
5. Certifications that appear repeatedly (CAMS, CIA, AWS Cloud Practitioner, CDMP, Google AI cert, etc.)

## Research Questions — Part B: Competitor Profile Analysis

1. Search LinkedIn for professionals holding AI Governance Manager, Responsible AI Lead, Head of AI Risk at target companies
2. Analyze how they describe their experience (language patterns, framing)
3. What career paths preceded AI governance?
4. How do they position technical vs. governance credentials?
5. Patterns to emulate or differentiate from

## Research Questions — Part C: Industry-Specific Norms

1. What do Big 4 AI advisory practices look for in resumes?
2. What do bank Chief AI/Data Offices look for in governance hires?
3. How do FS resume norms differ from tech?
4. Which regulatory knowledge should be prominently featured?

## Source Targets

- Current job descriptions from: EY, Deloitte, PwC, KPMG, JPMC, Citi, Goldman, Wells Fargo, Capital One, BNY Mellon
- LinkedIn profiles of people in target roles at target companies
- NIST AI RMF documentation
- SR 11-7 OCC guidance

## Deliverable

Validation pass: test accumulated recommendations against real-world job descriptions and competitor profiles. Flag any adjustments needed.
