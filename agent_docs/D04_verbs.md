# D4: Action Verb Selection & Sentence Tone

**Priority:** 6th | **Depends on:** D7, D3

## Research Questions

1. Highest-impact verbs for senior governance/strategy positioning?
2. Verbs signaling leadership vs. individual contributor work?
3. Verbs to avoid (overused, passive, weak)?
4. Verb tense across current and past roles?
5. Balance of "commanding" verbs (Led, Directed) vs. "strategic" verbs (Designed, Defined)?
6. Verb repetition tolerance — how many times before it weakens the resume?

## Verb Categories to Validate

- **Governance/Framework:** Designed, Architected, Established, Defined, Codified, Institutionalized
- **Leadership:** Led, Directed, Oversaw, Orchestrated, Guided, Mobilized
- **Alignment:** Aligned, Coordinated, Brokered, Facilitated, Navigated, Bridged
- **Change:** Drove, Transformed, Operationalized, Embedded, Sustained, Championed
- **Regulatory:** Ensured, Enforced, Remediated, Validated, Documented, Attested
- **Analysis:** Assessed, Evaluated, Analyzed, Diagnosed, Mapped, Benchmarked
- **Communication:** Presented, Communicated, Briefed, Reported, Articulated, Synthesized
